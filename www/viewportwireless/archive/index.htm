Know where

Using small, attachable sensors, your assets can send you automatic alerts, based on parameters that you choose.  So now you can know instantly if a service van is on its proper route, if important equipment is at the right job site, or even if a door or window is opened when it should be closed.

 

Know how

Our sensors can also alert you about vital performance metrics—including vibration, temperature, humidity, and run-times.  This can help you schedule maintenance, catch small problems before they become big ones, and avoid catastrophic breakdowns.

 

Know always

Our sensors are small enough to be attached to almost any valuable asset, and are powered by a battery that runs up to two years without recharging.  Yet the signal is strong enough to be detected when the equipment is located either indoors or outdoors.
